Weightloss Drink

This drink helps to reduce belly fat

Drink twice a day (morning & night) for 2 weeks for best results.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups of water
  • 1/2 squeezed lemon
  • 1 cup pineapples
  • 1 cup cucumbers
  • ginger
  • chia seeds

Steps

  1. Cut up pineapples and cucumbers and add it to a blender
  2. Add a piece of ginger to the blender
  3. Pour 2 cups of water in the blender
  4. Sqeeze a half of a lemon and add chia seeds
  5. Finally blend your inegredients and enjoy!
